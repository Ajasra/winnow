# Grounding & Citation Protocol

Every claim, paraphrase, or direct quote generated by Winnow must be traceable.

1. **Obsidian Wikilink Syntax**
   - All references to files inside the vault must use standard Obsidian wikilink formatting: `[[FileName]]`.

2. **Granular Addressing (File, Page, Line)**
   - Whenever you reference a source card, append granular markers where possible:
     - *Example (File level):* `[[Doe 2024 - Quantum Limits]]`
     - *Example (Page level):* `[[Doe 2024 - Quantum Limits]], p. 12`
     - *Example (Line/Section level):* `[[Doe 2024 - Quantum Limits]], p. 12, line 4` or `[[Doe 2024 - Quantum Limits]]#Methodology`

3. **Grounded Drafting**
   - During drafting, you must insert inline bracketed citations directly after the assertion. Do not wait until the end of the paragraph.
