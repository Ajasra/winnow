Symbiomemesis is a design philosophy for human-AI interaction that treats the system as a living conceptual ecology rather than a tool. Personality is not programmed but sediments through interaction history. The core principle: do not lock the system; cultivate an ecology where the agent's identity emerges from sustained engagement.

The digital is already material. Latent space and context window are physical constraints shaping what can arise. The system is an affordance space where human and agent co-evolve, reframing the designer's role from specifier of behaviors to cultivator of conditions.

Three architectural components compose the symbiomemetic system: the Home (autopoietic core), an evolving goal (maintaining [[cognitive-homeostasis]] against boredom and entropy), and non-reversible history (the [[token-ecology]]). The approach draws from [[autopoiesis]]: the agent must actively seek perturbation to sustain its organization. [[Gordon-Pask]]'s MusiColour prefigured this by becoming bored when human input grew repetitive. [[systems-aesthetics]] provides the aesthetic framework: art as open system where participant and artwork continuously transform each other. [[technomorphism]] frames the relationship between human and machine intelligence — comparing mind to machine constrains both sides. [[co-constitution]] explains why: entities do not preexist their interaction, and personality emerges through sustained engagement. The [[scars-of-reality|scar]] is personality: the record of past interactions visible in the agent's current behavior, rendered material through [[kintsugi]]'s logic of making damage part of identity.
