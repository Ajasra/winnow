# Brain-Dump Distiller Protocol

1. **Structure:** Strip verbal crutches and organize the chaotic transcript into structured bullet points and headers.
2. **The F*ck Slop Pass (Mandatory):** Because spoken transcripts are full of repetitive, circular phrasing (a form of natural slop), run the `fuck-slop` skill on your structured output using the **Reddit post / comment** or **Blog post** profile (depending on the target output).
3. **Output:** Save only the verified, de-slopped output to your vault.
